Mutton Biryani

Back to Home

Ingredients

  • 500g mutton, cut into pieces
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tbsp biryani masala
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • Saffron soaked in warm milk
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Marinate the mutton with yogurt, biryani masala, ginger-garlic paste, and salt. Refrigerate for 2 hours.
  2. Cook the basmati rice until 70% done. Drain and set aside.
  3. Heat ghee in a large pot, fry onions until golden brown, and set aside.
  4. In the same pot, cook the marinated mutton until tender.
  5. Layer the partially cooked rice over the mutton, drizzle with saffron milk, and garnish with fried onions, coriander, and mint leaves.
  6. Cover and cook on low heat for 20 minutes (Dum cooking).
  7. Serve hot with raita or a side salad.
