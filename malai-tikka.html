Chicken Tandoori

Back to Home

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp red chili powder
  • 1 tbsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Butter for basting

Instructions

  1. Score the chicken pieces and rub with lemon juice and salt. Set aside for 30 minutes.
  2. Mix yogurt, red chili powder, garam masala, ginger-garlic paste, and turmeric to form a marinade.
  3. Coat the chicken with the marinade and refrigerate for at least 4 hours.
  4. Preheat the oven to 220Â°C. Place the chicken on a baking tray and bake for 25-30 minutes.
  5. Baste the chicken with butter halfway through cooking for extra flavor.
  6. Serve hot with onion rings and lemon wedges.
