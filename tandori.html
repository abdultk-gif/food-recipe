Malai Chicken Tikka

Back to Home

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken pieces
  • 100g hung curd
  • 50g fresh cream
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp green chili paste
  • Salt to taste
  • Skewers for grilling

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken with lemon juice, salt, and ginger-garlic paste. Leave it for 30 minutes.
  2. Prepare the second marinade with hung curd, fresh cream, green chili paste, and salt.
  3. Coat the chicken pieces in this marinade and refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
  4. Preheat the grill and thread the chicken onto skewers.
  5. Grill the chicken on medium heat until golden brown, turning occasionally.
  6. Serve hot with mint chutney.
